# ☁️ Hybrid Cloud & Datacenter Modernization (2012–2021)

### **Strategic Overview**
This repository documents a 9-year transformation journey from high-density physical infrastructure to cloud-native architecture. As a **Lead Consultant & Architect** at **DXC Technology**, I orchestrated the evolution of legacy environments into modern, hyperconverged, and cloud-integrated systems for global enterprise clients.

---

### **🏗️ Core Architectural Pillars**

* **Disaster Recovery & Migration (Zerto):** Subject Matter Expert (SME) for **Zerto Replication** implementation. Architected and executed high-availability Disaster Recovery (DR) solutions and large-scale migrations with near-zero RTO/RPO requirements.
* **Hyperconverged Infrastructure (HCI):** Directed the 2-year lifecycle management of **Dell EMC VxRack and VxRail** solutions, transitioning the organization to a software-defined datacenter (SDDC) model.
* **Global Infrastructure Deployment:** Spearheaded on-site datacenter server infrastructure builds across multiple **USA locations**, managing the end-to-end physical-to-virtual (P2V) migration and hardware provisioning.
* **Cloud Solutions Architecture:** Promoted to **Architect**, providing the strategic blueprint for migrating legacy on-premises workloads to **Microsoft Azure**.
* **Modernization Strategy:** Managed 2+ years of complex P2V migrations, successfully decommissioning legacy hardware and consolidating workloads into high-efficiency virtualized clusters.

---

### **🚀 The "Evolution" Bridge**
The rigor of managing **Zerto for Disaster Recovery** across international datacenters is the direct precursor to managing **Stateful Workloads** in **OpenShift**. Understanding how to protect data during a migration is a critical skill for modern Cloud Architects.
